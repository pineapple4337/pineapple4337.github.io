Bamboo Diet

The "Thumb"

Pandas have an enlarged wrist bone that functions like a thumb to help them grip bamboo stalks.

Why are they black and white?

Scientists believe the panda's unique markings serve as camouflage. The white face and neck help them hide in snow, while the black arms and legs help them hide in deep shade forest shadows.
